# âš ï¸ Risk & Contingencies

## Technical Risks
- **Risk:** Virtual mode harder than expected
  - **Mitigation:** Start with simple P2P, fall back to pass-and-play
  - **Timeline Impact:** Could delay launch by 2-4 weeks

- **Risk:** Native apps take longer (first time Swift/Kotlin)
  - **Mitigation:** Launch web first, apps are nice-to-have
  - **Timeline Impact:** Move apps to Q3 if needed

- **Risk:** Performance issues at scale
  - **Mitigation:** Monitor closely, optimize early
  - **Timeline Impact:** Could require 1-2 week sprint

## Market Risks
- **Risk:** Low user adoption
  - **Mitigation:** Strong marketing, beta testing, iterate on feedback
  - **Response:** Double down on marketing, add most-requested features

- **Risk:** Competitor launches similar product
  - **Mitigation:** Launch quickly, focus on free + quality
  - **Response:** Differentiate on price (free), UX, community

## Resource Risks
- **Risk:** Solo developer burnout
  - **Mitigation:** Realistic timelines, take breaks, celebrate wins
  - **Response:** Reduce scope, extend timeline, recruit help

# ğŸ¨ Flexibility Guidelines

**This roadmap is a living document.**

**Principles:**
1. **Ship fast, iterate:** Better to launch with 6 good games than wait for 10 perfect ones
2. **Listen to users:** If beta testers want X feature, adjust priorities
3. **Quality over quantity:** A few great games > many mediocre ones
4. **Momentum matters:** Keep shipping regularly, don't go dark
5. **Protect the vision:** Free, accessible, high-quality always

**When to adjust:**
- User feedback reveals different priorities
- Technical blockers require rethinking
- Opportunity for partnership/feature emerges
- Burnout risk - extend timeline
- Moving faster than expected - pull features forward
